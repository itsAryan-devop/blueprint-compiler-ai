Phase 8: failure handling - graceful on vague/conflicting/empty prompts

The task: messy prompts must NOT crash or guess silently. Either ask a
clarifying question or proceed with documented assumptions.

- pipeline/input_analysis.py: deterministic, code-only analyzer (no LLM). Looks
  at the raw prompt and classifies it as EMPTY / VAGUE / CONFLICTING /
  UNDERSPECIFIED / OK, returning reasons, ready-to-record assumptions, and a
  clarifying question when there is nothing to compile.
- pipeline/intent.py: extract_intent accepts prior_assumptions and weaves them
  into the prompt + guarantees they end up in IntentSpec.assumptions even if the
  model drops them. They flow through refine() into AppBlueprint.assumptions.
- pipeline/compiler.py: now returns a CompileResult. For EMPTY input it
  short-circuits with needs_clarification=True (no AI call); for everything else
  it injects the analyzer's assumptions and runs the pipeline.
- pipeline/__init__.py: export analyze_request, InputDiagnosis, Severity,
  CompileResult.
- run_pipeline.py + demo_determinism.py: updated for CompileResult.
- demo_failure_handling.py + tests/test_failure_handling.py: cover empty, too
  short, vague, two conflicts, underspecified, and a clean prompt.
- README: Phase 8 done.

All 27 unit tests pass. Zero AI calls -- quota-friendly.
